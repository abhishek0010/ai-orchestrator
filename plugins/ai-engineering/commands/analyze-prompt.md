Analyze an AI prompt for clarity, effectiveness, and potential issues.

Load the following expertise before starting:

- [prompt-engineering](../../../skills/prompt-engineering/SKILL.md)

## Process

1. Evaluate the prompt against core criteria:
   - Clarity of instructions.
   - Specificity of the objective.
   - Completeness of the context.
   - Usage of examples (few-shotting).
   - Prompt structure (delimiters, hierarchies).
2. Identify potential issues:
   - Ambiguity, hallmark AI filler, bias, or lack of constraints.
3. Assess token efficiency.
4. Provide a report with scores and specific areas for improvement.

## Rules

- Strictly follow [prompt-engineering](../../../skills/prompt-engineering/SKILL.md) best practices.
- Check for "hallucination triggers" (e.g., asking for facts without context).
- Ensure the prompt has clear success criteria.
